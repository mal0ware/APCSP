A computing innovation is something that can't have been accomplished without the use of computers and computer science.
Some examples of computer innovation are: A smartwatch, iPhone, Roomba robot, Electric Drumsets and Alexa AI. These have been built using a program or computer. 
Some examples of things that arent computing innovations are a banana, the ocean, the sun, a worm, and george washington. As you can see, these are things that did exist without computers.
