BY means credit must be given to the creator.
SA means adaptions must be shared under the same terms
NC means only noncommercial uses are permitted.
ND means no derivatives or adaptations are permitted.
The least restrictive is the CC BY because all you have to do is give credit to the author.
The most restrictive then, is the CC BY-NC-ND because you have to give credit to the creatorm you cant use it commmercially and you cant make adaptaions.
